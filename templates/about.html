About EduRipple

EduRipple empowers Kenyan teachers with AI-driven tools to simplify CBC curriculum planning and create impactful learning resources.

✓ Teacher-first design ✓ CBC aligned ✓ Built for real classrooms

Our Mission

To revolutionize education in Kenya by providing teachers with intelligent, accessible tools that reduce planning burden and increase instructional quality. We believe every Kenyan student deserves teachers who have time to teach, not just plan.

Education-First Approach

Every feature is designed to reduce teacher workload and improve classroom preparation quality. We listen to educators and build solutions that matter.

KICD CBC Aligned

Generated outputs are organized around strands, learning outcomes, core competencies, and values. Fully compliant with Kenya's CBC curriculum framework.

Built by Teachers

EduRipple is shaped by real school needs—simple, practical, and supportive for daily teaching. Our team understands classroom realities.
